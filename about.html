विविधा

The Art Club of NIIT University, NU विविधा is an active club of the university which focuses on showcasing different art forms from various perspective. विविधा derived from the hindi word विविध, as the word speaks for itself meaning- ‘various’ or ‘varying’. It symbolizes all works and art forms shared by the members of the club of different places, genres, themes and ideas. NU विविधा Art Club symbolizes integration and enhancement of all the diverse and independent art forms. NU विविधा Art Club intends to endorse- ‘Any thought in one’s mind can be expressed through a painting’. This enables a variety of topics woven with beautiful art forms. The artists challenge themselves and come up with beautiful characters, landscapes, sculptures, structures, miniatures, origami and craft artefacts. The club has an exclusive gallery for the artists to express anything, anytime in any form of ART with all the material available there. Various mediums are used by our artists for craftsmanship like charcoal, pencil, pastels, clay, oil or acrylic paints, crafting charts, colors and various types of papers. All are available at ease to transform and bring changes to the picturesque gallery. Earlier, NU विविधा Art Club was limited to Fine Arts based on the interest and passion of the students. However, recent technological advancements made the artists to look beyond the existing art forms. As a result, some members have been working in Digital Arts that uses various design software and has the applications in digital media. Eventually, Digital Art has been formally recognised in विविधा. The University holds an exhibition every year for विविधा to unveil the artistic talent innate in the students. The exhibitions showcase artists’ imagination and creativity to the world through an interesting theme that audience would love to have a glimpse of. विविधा aims to send a message to the society and inspire it through the artefacts. NU विविधा Art Club still focuses on expanding to a larger level where the ways of expression know no bounds!
